# Verity JE

**Verity JE** is an official adaptation of the **Verity** property, bringing the AI-powered assistant from ThatMob's video into **Minecraft: Java Edition**.

Powered by AI through **Groq**, Verity is designed to act as your intelligent companion throughout your Minecraft world. He can talk with you, help you, react to your actions, and introduce unique gameplay mechanics inspired by the original video.

This project is **officially licensed** and is published with **explicit permission and contractual agreement from ThatMob**.

---

# Features

## 🤖 Verity

Meet **Verity**, your personal AI-powered helper. Interact with him naturally through voice or text, ask questions, have conversations, or simply let him accompany you during your adventures.

---

## 📦 The Box

Verity arrives inside his own **fully animated box**, faithfully recreated from the original concept.

The box serves as Verity's home and spawning point, complete with animations and interactions.

---

## 👹 Verity Demon

Be careful...

Depending on what happens during gameplay, Verity may summon—or become connected to—the **Verity Demon**, a terrifying entity inspired by ThatMob's original video.

---

## 🌑 Gameplay Changes

Installing Verity JE changes much more than simply adding an AI companion.

Some gameplay mechanics include:

* Extreme darkness
* Atmospheric visual changes
* New scripted events
* Unique encounters
* Horror-inspired mechanics
* Additional surprises throughout your playthrough

These changes are designed to recreate the feeling and atmosphere seen in **ThatMob's Verity video**.

---

## 🌎 Language Support

### AI Chat

The AI itself supports **all languages**.

### Speech-to-Text

Currently tested and supported in:

* English

### Text-to-Speech

Currently tested and supported in:

* English

Other languages may work, but only English has been officially tested.

# Installation

## Step 1

Download the latest release from this repository.

---

## Step 2

Open the **CurseForge** launcher.

---

## Step 3

Create or select the Minecraft profile you want to use.

---

## Step 4

Click the **⋮ (three dots)** on your profile and choose:

**Open Folder**

This opens your Minecraft instance folder.

---

## Step 5

Open the **mods** folder.

If it doesn't exist, create one.

---

## Step 6

Copy the downloaded Verity JE mod files into the **mods** folder.

---

## ⚠️ Important

**Do NOT only download the main mod file.**

Go to the **Releases** page and click **"All Files."**

Download **every required file** listed there.

**The mod will NOT function correctly unless every required file from "All Files" is installed.**

Missing files can cause:

* Verity not spawning
* AI features failing
* Missing sounds
* Missing animations
* Missing assets
* Other unexpected issues

If something seems broken, the first thing to check is whether **all files** have been downloaded.

---

## Step 7

Launch Minecraft using the CurseForge profile.

If everything was installed correctly, Verity will be ready to use.
